FAQ

  • How can I join the club?
    • You can join the LEGO building club by submitting your application on the CIT's clubs website or on our website at the membership page.
  • I'm new to LEGOs, can I still join?
    • Of course, our club is open to people with all levels of LEGO building knowledge; be it beginner or expert. All are welcome to join!
  • Do I need to have LEGOs?
    • We understand that most of you don't have LEGOs to build with. If you are a beginner with a sparkling interest for LEGOs, we are here for you to help you choose the perfect LEGO build for you.
